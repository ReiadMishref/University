Values

Kafr El Sheikh University is a government educational institution that seeks to produce, integrate, apply and disseminate scientific knowledge by building a competitive advantage in the fields of education, research.

Goals

Providing a stimulating environment for teaching, learning, and intellectual creativity, preparing distinguished graduates who are qualified to meet the needs of the labor market, and to provide distinguished community services.

University history

Kafr El Sheikh University was established by Republic Decree No. (129) on April 19, 2006 AD, and historically the nucleus of the university's colleges began at the Higher Agricultural Institute in 1957 AD in Kafr.

About US

The university contains nineteen colleges and three higher institutes, consisting of five scientific colleges: agriculture, engineering, science, fisheries and fisheries sciences, computers and information, and seven health colleges: medicine, pharmacy, dentistry, physiotherapy, nursing, and the College of Medical and Applied Sciences, in addition to the college Veterinary Medicine.

University's president

Dr. Abdel-Razek Youssef Abdel-Aziz Dessouki graduated from the Faculty of Veterinary Medicine, Zagazig University, in 1988 AD, and obtained a master's degree in 1992, and received a doctorate from Zagazig University in 1998

Desouky was appointed as a teaching assistant at the Faculty of Veterinary Medicine at Zagazig University, in 1988 AD, then assistant teacher in 1992 AD, and promoted as a teacher at the Faculty of Veterinary Medicine, Kafr El Sheikh University in 1998 AD, then assistant professor at the same university, in 2004
